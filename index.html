About

Welcome to Nocturnal RP

Nocturnal RP is a immersive FiveM roleplay server where the night comes alive. We offer a serious roleplay experience with a focus on quality interactions, engaging storylines, and a welcoming community.

Built on the QB Core framework, our server delivers a stable, feature-rich environment with custom scripts, realistic economies, and endless opportunities for both new and veteran roleplayers.

Whether you're looking to build a life as a civilian, uphold the law, or walk the fine line between, Nocturnal RP is your home in the shadows.

What We Offer

Everything you need for an unforgettable roleplay experience

Civilian Life

Build your story from the ground up. Choose from dozens of jobs, own property, run businesses, and create the life you've always dreamed of in Los Santos.

Law Enforcement

Protect and serve with our detailed LSPD and BCSO departments. Complete with realistic uniforms, vehicles, protocols, and a fair chain of command.
